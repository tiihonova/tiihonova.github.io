What are playable ads?

Playable ads are interactive advertisements in the form of small games, that allow players to preview the app and test its gameplay before installing the main game. After beating the mini-game, players are encouraged to download the full version of the game from the app store. Also it can redirect players to a website or display any other call to action.

how are they built?

As Playable Ads need to be very lightweight and load almost instantly, they are built with HTML5, on such engines as Pixi.js, Cocos, Phaser.js and others. Playable Ads for different advertising platforms like Facebook, Google Play have different build specifications, guidelines and
technical limitations.

how do playable ads work ?

Playable Ads are HTML5 mini-games that allow players to get a taste of gameplay of the main app with a few taps.

Because Playable Ads allow test-driving the game before downloading it, they effectively filter out non-engaged users, which leads to more high quality player installs and improved ROAS (return on ad spend).

Key components of a Playable Ad

01

tutorial

Super-short (3-5 seconds) introduction of how to play the game. It should explain core mechanics, be immersive, simple and intuitive. Any instructions should be clear and to the point.

02

gameplay

Main part where potential players will try out the game, they should be persuaded in under 20-30 seconds to download your app. Creating a powerful hook like the end of an exciting level, or putting them right in the thick of a fight will help.

03

end card

The end card (also called packshot screen) should display a clear call to action (CTA), asking the player to install the game, redirect to a particular website or perform other actions.
